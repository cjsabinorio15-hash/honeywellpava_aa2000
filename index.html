UNDERSTANDING THE PRODUCT
What Is a PAVA System?

A PAVA (Public Address and Voice Alarm) system is the certified life-safety voice communication backbone of any commercial building. It broadcasts emergency evacuation instructions, fire alerts, and general announcements — instantly, clearly, and to every floor simultaneously.

Unlike a basic intercom or background music system, a PAVA system is purpose-built to meet strict regulatory standards. In the Philippines, commercial buildings are required to comply with BFP regulations, NFPA 72, and BS EN 54-16 — all of which mandate certified, reliable voice alarm systems.

The Honeywell PAVA System meets every one of those requirements — backed by Honeywell's 100+ years of life-safety engineering expertise and a global support network.

Buildings without a certified PAVA system are non-compliant — and at serious risk in an emergency.
